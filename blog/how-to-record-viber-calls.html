How to Record Viber Calls in 2026

Disclosure: Mono is our product. We've included it in this guide and tried to be fair, but you should know we have a stake in it.

Viber Desktop doesn't include a built-in call recording feature. If you need to save voice or video calls from Viber on your computer — whether for business records, interviews, or personal memories — you'll need a third-party recording tool.

Here are three methods that work reliably for recording Viber calls in 2026.

TL;DR

  • Best free: OBS Studio — requires setup, no transcription

Method 1: Mono (Recommended)

Mono is a desktop app that records any audio playing through your computer, including Viber calls. It captures audio directly from your system's sound output — nothing is visible to the other person on the call. After recording, Mono automatically transcribes the conversation using local AI.

How to record Viber calls with Mono

  1. Download Mono from mono-ai.uk and install it
  2. Open Mono and click the Record button
  3. Start your Viber call
  4. When the call ends, click Stop in Mono
  5. View your transcript with speaker labels and timestamps

Pros: Works with Viber voice and video calls, invisible to other participants, automatic transcription with speaker identification, one-time $50 payment.

Cons: Paid software (one free recording to try).

Method 2: OBS Studio (Free)

OBS Studio is free, open-source software primarily used for streaming and screen recording. It can capture system audio, making it suitable for recording Viber calls.

How to record Viber calls with OBS

  1. Download OBS Studio from obsproject.com
  2. Open Settings → Audio
  3. Set "Desktop Audio" to your playback device
  4. Add an Audio Output Capture source
  5. Click "Start Recording" before your Viber call
  6. Click "Stop Recording" when the call ends

Pros: Completely free, open source, can record video if needed.

Cons: Requires initial configuration, manual start/stop, no transcription.

Tips for Better Viber Call Recordings

Use headphones to avoid echo. Close unnecessary apps that might produce sounds. Check your audio levels with a test recording before important calls.

Which Method Should You Use?

If you need transcription: Mono is the only option that includes automatic speech-to-text.

If you want free: OBS Studio works but requires setup and doesn't transcribe.

For regular use: Mono's one-click recording is more convenient than manually starting OBS before each call.
